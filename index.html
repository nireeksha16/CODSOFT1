About Details

Safety

Safety is primary goal at Frolic in the Boonies and we are proud of our safety record. We have worked with over 300 students in the last 3 years without any hospital stays or serious injuries. We teach our participants to develop “expedition behaviour” and our experienced instructors teach safety in every activity. Medical safety - We have first aid kits available in our campus for minor injuries. Doctor on call - We have a tie up with a local doctor. Nearest hospital is 7 km away. Children are under supervision throughout the day by camp counsellors Children wear Frolic Boonies Uniform and identity cards during out of the campus activities like nature trails, jungle safaris, etc
